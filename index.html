THE COMPANY

The history of the brands Hocosa and Sawaco dates back in 1886, in the Swiss city of Winterthur. Since then the industrial reality and the brands have climbed through nearly 150 years, and crossed the threshold of the new millennium, always remaining in Switzerland, always taking the quality of the product as the compass of its journey.
Since ist very beginning, both Hocosa and Sawaco wer associated with the finest Swiss textile industry, with a specific focus on underwear.

THE PRODUCTS

Hocosa SA wants to offer to its customers products that are made of the best possible material, BIO, created without the use of chemicals or syntetics. We use manufacturing processes that respect the nature and the environment.
Our products are made in:

100% Merino Wool,
70% merino Wool 30% Silk,
45% Cotton organic 35% merino Wool 20% Silk,
Silk Tussah 100%.

Our lines are made for Women, Men, Sport, Children and Babys.


QUALITY-SWITZERLAND

A tight control on all the sourcing and manufacturing process aims to ensure the best quality of the raw materials used to craft our products. Our fabric is all purchased from certified suppliers and every step in the process is carefully monitored. The production site of Hocosa is in Bedano, Switzerland. Switzerland is globally associated to concepts like quality, precision, reliability and we want these qualities to be perceived in our products.
We created a network of partners in Europe (Germany, Austria, Italy) that follow us in providing products of the highest quality.


NATURAL-BIO

Our products do not undergo any chemical or synthesis treatment. We select our raw materials in full respect of nature and environment, indeed our wool is obtained only by manual sheep shearing.
There is much discussion surrounding the numerous BIO certifications available, and if the reputation of a certification is based on the difficulty of obtaining it, then GOTS deserves its recognition as one of the most reliable certifications in this field.
It's not just about the required documentation and strict monitoring, but also the care and attention the GOTS organization gives to those seeking certification through regulations and systematic audits. Hocosa is particularly proud to hold the GOTS certification for decades, with all our wool/wool-silk/wool-silk-cotton products being GOTS certified.
